Join

We are always looking for students committed to fostering an inclusive and welcoming environment and to amplify the voices of underrpresented groups in Applied Computation.

The GAC is a unique opportunity for IACS students to gain diversity and inclusion leadership within the academic domain.

Who can join?

Membership is open to all IACS affiliates. This includes, but is not exclusive to, current students and alumni.

What is the time commitment?

We ask a minimum time commitment of five hours a month, including two bi-weekly meetings, and sitting on at least one subcommitte:

  • Admissions, Recruiting, & Funding
  • Career Development & Mentorship
  • Community Building
  • Historically Minoritized Student Support
  • International Student Support
  • Pedagogy

How do I join?

You can join the GAC through GSAS Engage and by emailing us at iacsgac [a] gmail [dot] com. This ensures that you will receive all our meeting and event invites.

If you are considering joining and would like to join us during our bi-weekly meeting to meet the team and see how we work, please see our GAC Meetings for our schedule.
